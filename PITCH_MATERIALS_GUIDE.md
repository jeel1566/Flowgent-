## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### **Humor Calibration:**
- **Opening:** Light humor to warm up room ("learning experiences")
- **Middle:** Strategic analogies (GPS, Ferrari with Japanese manual)
- **Numbers sections:** Keep it professional (let data speak)
- **Closing:** Warm, confident, memorable (coffee offer)

### **Demo Strategy:**
- Show, don't just tell (live demo beats screenshots)
- Have backup video ready (tech fails happen)
- Keep demos SHORT (30-60 seconds each feature)
- Narrate what you're doing ("Watch this...")

### **Handling Skepticism:**
- Acknowledge it ("Great question!")
- Provide data ("Here's what happened to Makerpad...")
- Offer to show more ("Can I pull up the GitHub?")
- Stay confident ("I appreciate the skepticismâ€”that's smart investing")

### **Reading the Room:**
- Technical audience = dive deeper into architecture
- Business audience = focus on revenue model
- Mixed audience = use analogies to bridge gap
- Engaged audience = invite questions throughout
- Skeptical audience = show more data, less story
